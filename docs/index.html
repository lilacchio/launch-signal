What it produces

💡 The Momentum Attachment Framework: Most new projects try to start their own conversation. launch-signal finds the conversation already happening in Solana Twitter and helps you insert your project as the answer.

founder-freq

Solana Founder Personal Brand Playbook. Five founder archetypes distilled from studying the biggest builders in the ecosystem. Identifies your primary + secondary blend and builds a complete voice, content, and growth strategy around it.

What it produces

  • ✓Founder archetype analysis with primary and secondary blend explained
  • ✓Unique POV: the one specific angle to own on X
  • ✓3-4 content pillars with post frequency per pillar
  • ✓Writing style guide: tone, sentence structure, jargon rules, phrases to use and avoid
  • ✓Weekly posting rhythm calibrated to your archetype
  • ✓Cold-start strategy for founders under 1,000 followers
  • ✓10 fully written example posts, ready to paste

The Five Founder Archetypes

Every output is calibrated to a primary and secondary archetype blend. Not a template — a system built from studying what actually works for each type.

🔭
Technical Oracle
Rare, dense posts. Every one carries weight. Growth through reputation compound interest.
🕸
Ecosystem Builder
Connective tissue of the ecosystem. Celebrates and amplifies others. Growth through reciprocity loops.
⚡
Contrarian
Takes strong positions. Debates publicly. Growth because controversy is distribution.
🏗
Builder-in-Public
Documents everything, ships constantly. Growth because people root for the journey.
📊
Strategist
Predicts markets, reads trends. Growth through track record of being right early.

🎨 Writing Style Guide
Tone: Confident and specific. States conclusions before explaining them. Never hedges with "I think" or "it seems like." Brings receipts.

Sentence structure: Short declarative statements for claims. Longer structured sentences for explanation. Never uses bullet points in regular posts — thread structure instead, one idea per tweet.

Phrases to use: "Here's the data:" / "The real problem isn't X, it's Y" / "Most people are thinking about this wrong because" / "We tested this. Here's what happened."

Phrases to avoid: "Exciting to share that..." / "WAGMI" / "The future of finance is..." / "Thoughts?" as a post ending.
