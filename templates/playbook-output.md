## 4-Week Content Plan

### Week 1: Establish Existence

| Day | Post Type | Notes |
|-----|-----------|-------|
| Day 1 | Launch thread | Use Post 1 below |
| Day 3 | The problem | No product mention |
| Day 5 | Team intro | Why this problem chose you |
| Day 7 | Technical mechanism | One insight, explained simply |

### Week 2: Build Credibility

- 2x posts: something building this taught you that surprised you
- 1x engagement: specific question to your target users
- Daily: reply to 3 threads in your category, add one insight, no pitch

### Week 3: Build Community

- 1x behind-the-scenes: actual work, not the polished result
- 1x collaboration: find an adjacent project to amplify
- 1x milestone: what shipped, what's next
- 1x X Space: contribute a specific point, not a product mention

### Week 4: Convert

- 1x results thread: shipped, who's using it, what you learned
- 1x macro post: why this matters for Solana
- 1x direct CTA: specific ask, one link, one action
